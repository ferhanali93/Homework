About Ferhan Ali


Ferhan Ali is an upcoming Quality Assurance Automation Engineer with a Bachelors Degree in Accounting from the university of New York. He loves to learn new and interesting things that make his work easier and effective. He has always been dedicated and hardworking when trying to achieve my goals. He takes his very day as a new challenge and always stay ahead of every situations he comes across. It can be school or work. He doesn't give up easily, He will work for it and make it happen.

Upon completion of my Associate degree in Accounting from LaGuardia community college, I enrolled myself to peruse Bachelor degree in Accounting in the well known business college of Queens. The foundation which I built in LaGuardia community college gave me the strength to my education in Queens college. In the mean time I will work as junior accountant in some nonprofit farm. This is my short term goal, so that I could develop my knowledge and enhance my skills, while being an effective asset to the company. My long term goal is to become a qualified CPA and be able to continue my contribution as CPA to the industry successfully. If I am able to rise above my competition and be able take a significant position in the company, I want to use that privilege to apply my knowledge and creativity to become the best in the industry. After completion of my Bachelor degree in Accounting, I will further my education towards becoming well qualified CPA from Baruch. After completion of 150 semester hours of a program of study in accounting. I will sit for the Uniform CPA Examination, which is developed and graded by the AICPA . After passing the exam I will like to have professional work experience in public accounting. There are Currently over 46,000 public accounting firms in the US and I want to pursue my career in one of these firms. I am very strong and passionate about Accounting and would like to excel it. Therefore, I am strongly willing to participate in some programs and activities which are needed to complete my goals in LaGuardia Community College as well as in my future institute. I am very committed to fulfilling my goals. I will push myself in all ways to succeed and graduate so that I may fulfill my dream and to be a proud employee at a large Accounting industry. To achieve high career growth through a continuous learning process and keep myself dynamic, visionary and competitive with the challenging scenario of the world.
